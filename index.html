About the event

Unlocking US-India's Trade Conference

The South Asia Center's “Unlocking US-India’s Trade Conference”, launched in partnership with the US Consulate, Chennai, will explore the economic partnership between the United States and India. The conference will bring together representatives from US, Indian, and international businesses and trade experts to enable direct interactions among global economic stakeholders, thought leaders, and policy makers aimed at curating sound economic policies to bolster trade.
